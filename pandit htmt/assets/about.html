ALDERT EINSTEIN

--- albert einstein was the great scientist ---

Albert Einstein became interested in science and mathematics when he was a young child. He wondered about the invisible forces that made a compass work and taught himself geometry by reading a book about it. A friend introduced him to books about mathematics, philosophy, and electricity.

Born: 14 March 1879, Ulm, Germany Died: 18 April 1955, Princeton, New Jersey, United States Education: University of Zurich (1905), ETH Zürich (1897–1900), Alte Kantonsschule Aarau (1895–1896), more Awards: Nobel Prize in Physics, Gold Medal of the Royal Astronomical Society, Copley Medal, more Spouse: Elsa Einstein (m. 1919–1936), Mileva Marić (m. 1903–1919) Height: 1.7 m Children: Eduard Einstein, Hans Albert Einstein, Lieserl Einstein
